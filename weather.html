Weather in Sri Lanka


Weather in Sri Lanka is quite unique as you may find it to be rather hot and warm on the months from January to May. After which it cools down a bit and rain can be expected in the months that follow, from June to November. Then in true seasonal fashion it gets much cooler. Please note that the weather and the overall temperature which change according to the district and the amount of wind. But you can always stay updated abou the weather in different districts by using the our website.


an instance districts suchs as Nuwara Eliya is normaly quite cold whole year around, which it is famous for. Being also reffered to as "Little England" Nuwara Eliya a phenominal and sensational, experience thanks to its chilli weather amongst the hot weather in other districts.
