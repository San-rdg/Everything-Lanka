FAQs

What is Sri Lanka's GDP growth in 2025?

Forecasts from major institutions place 2025 GDP growth between 3.0% and 3.5%.

Is Sri Lanka's economy recovering?

Yes — signs of recovery began in late 2023 and strengthened in 2024–2025.

What sectors lead growth in 2025?

Tourism, industrial exports, agriculture modernization, IT/BPO, and remittances.

What are the biggest challenges?

Debt restructuring, inflation management, and rebuilding investor confidence.
