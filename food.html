Must-Try Sri Lankan Dishes

Rice & Curry – A staple meal with a mix of vegetables, meat, or fish.

Kottu Roti – Famous street food made of chopped roti, eggs, and vegetables.

Hoppers – Bowl-shaped pancakes served plain or with eggs.

Popular Street Food

Isso Wade – Spicy prawn fritters, a must-try snack.

Short Eats – Tasty pastries like patties, cutlets, and fish buns.

Sri Lankan Drinks

Ceylon Tea – World-famous tea with a rich taste and aroma.

King Coconut – A refreshing natural drink.
