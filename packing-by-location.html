Frequently Asked Questions

Is this list suitable for all seasons?

Yes! Our suggestions are designed for year-round travel, but always check the local weather forecast before packing.

Do I need travel insurance?

Absolutely. We strongly recommend travel insurance to cover medical emergencies, trip cancellations, and more.

Is Sri Lanka safe for tourists?

Generally, yes. Most areas are safe, but it's always wise to follow common travel precautions and local advice.

Can I buy these items locally?

Many essentials like mosquito repellent and sunscreen are available in towns, but bringing your own ensures availability and quality.
