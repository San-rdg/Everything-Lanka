Top 10 Destinations

十大热门目的地

Colombo — City Life & Shopping科伦坡 — 城市生活与购物

Colombo is a lively hub for shopping, Chinese-friendly restaurants, and city tours. Ideal for short stays and easy international flight connections.

科伦坡是繁华的购物与美食中心，拥有不少友好的中餐餐馆，适合短暂停留并便于国际航班衔接。

Pexels photos: Colombo on Pexels

Galle Fort — Colonial Charm & Coastal Walks加勒古堡 — 殖民风情与海滨漫步

Historic streets, boutique shopping, and seaside sunsets make Galle Fort popular with photographers and couples.

古老街区、精品店和海边日落，非常适合摄影爱好者与情侣。

Pexels photos: Galle Fort on Pexels

Ella — Scenic Hills & Train Rides埃拉 — 山景与列车之旅

Famous for its dramatic train journey, Ella offers tea terraces, Little Adam's Peak, and relaxed cafés — perfect for photographers and nature lovers.

以壮丽的火车旅程著称，拥有茶园梯田、小亚当峰与舒适咖啡馆，适合自然与摄影爱好者。

Pexels photos: Ella on Pexels

Sigiriya — Ancient Rock Fortress狮子岩 — 古代岩石要塞

Climb the iconic Sigiriya rock for panoramic views and well-preserved frescoes. Best visited in the dry season (Dec–Apr).

攀登标志性狮子岩可观赏全景与壁画。旱季（12–4月）为最佳旅游时间。

Pexels photos: Sigiriya on Pexels

Kandy — Cultural Heart & Temple of the Tooth康提 — 文化中心与佛牙寺

Kandy is celebrated for the Temple of the Tooth, cultural shows, and cooler hill-city weather. It's popular with visitors who want cultural immersion.

康提以佛牙寺、文化表演与凉爽的高地气候著称，适合追求文化体验的游客。

Pexels photos: Kandy on Pexels

Nuwara Eliya — Cool Tea Country努沃勒埃利耶 — 清凉的茶乡

Known as "Little England", Nuwara Eliya has scenic tea estates, colonial-era charm, and cool climates — attractive to tourists seeking scenic drives and tea tours.

被称作“小英格兰”，有风景如画的茶园、殖民风情与凉爽气候，适合自驾与茶园参观。

Pexels photos: Nuwara Eliya on Pexels

Yala National Park — Wildlife Safaris雅拉国家公园 — 野生动物狩猎观赏

One of the best places to spot leopards, elephants, and rich birdlife. Choose an experienced guide and morning safaris for higher wildlife activity.

是观察花豹、大象与丰富鸟类的好去处。建议选择经验丰富的向导并参加早晨游猎以提高观赏几率。

Pexels photos: Yala on Pexels

Mirissa — Whale Watching & Beaches米瑞沙 — 观鲸与海滩

Mirissa is known for whale-watching tours (seasonal), relaxed beaches, and fresh seafood — a favorite for marine experiences.

米瑞沙以观鲸（有季节性）、宁静海滩与海鲜著称，是海上体验的热门选择。

Pexels photos: Mirissa on Pexels

Bentota — Beaches & Water Sports本托塔 — 海滩与水上活动

Bentota is a well-developed beach hub with water sports, family-friendly resorts, and good connections to Colombo.

本托塔是成熟的海滨目的地，提供水上活动、亲子度假村并与科伦坡交通便利。

Pexels photos: Bentota on Pexels

Dambulla — Cave Temples & Heritage丹布拉 — 石窟寺与文化遗产

Dambulla Cave Temple features ancient murals and statues. Often paired with Sigiriya and Polonnaruwa on cultural circuits.

丹布拉石窟寺有古老壁画与佛像，常与狮子岩、波隆纳鲁沃组合为文化线路参观。

Pexels photos: Dambulla on Pexels

Quick travel tips for Chinese visitors

给中国游客的旅行小贴士

Best seasons: December–April for west/south coasts and hill country; May–September for the east coast. Bring local currency (LKR), consider a local SIM, and select guides who can serve Chinese-language groups if needed.

最佳季节：西南海岸与高地为12–4月，东海岸为5–9月。请携带本地货币（卢比），可购买本地SIM卡，若需要可选择会中文的导游或旅行社。

Quick FAQ

常见问题

Visa & entry
Most visitors require an ETA or visa — check the Sri Lanka official immigration site before travel.
大多数访客需要电子旅行许可或签证，出发前请查阅斯里兰卡移民官网。
Local language
Sinhala and Tamil are official; English is widely used in tourism. For Chinese-language support, book guides or agencies that advertise Chinese services.
僧伽罗语与泰米尔语为官方语言，旅游业中英语广泛使用。如需中文服务，请预订提供中文的导游或旅行社。
