Public Transport

Sri Lanka offers various public transport options including trains and buses that provide affordable and scenic travel.

Private & Rental Options

Tuk-tuks, taxis, and rental cars are popular among travelers looking for more flexibility.

Domestic Flights & Helicopter Services

For long-distance travel, domestic flights and helicopter charters are available.

Tips for Travelers

Be aware of local transport pricing, always negotiate with tuk-tuk drivers, and book train tickets in advance.
