India is one of the largest sources of travellers to Sri Lanka. The process is simple and fast:

  • Indian travellers require an ETA to enter Sri Lanka.
  • A valid passport with 6 months minimum validity is required.
  • Return ticket & proof of accommodation may be checked at immigration.
  • No vaccination certificates required unless stated by airlines.

üõÇ Can You Travel to Sri Lanka Without a Passport?

No. A valid passport is mandatory for entry into Sri Lanka. Emergency travel documents may be accepted for Sri Lankan citizens returning home, but foreign tourists cannot enter without a valid passport.

  • Visa-on-arrival during special travel campaigns
