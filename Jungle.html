Jungle Beach, Sri Lanka

The jungle beach an exotic beach found in Galle. It is very clean and utterly beautiful with its crystal clear sands and breathtaking view.

Top Attractions

  • Clean, sandy, low tide beach.
  • Amazing view of Galle town.
  • Aswome atmosphere and party vide.
  • Boat rides.

How to Get There

  • Located in **Galle District**, Sri Lanka.
  • Accessible via the **personal vehicle(car)**from the Galle town.
  • Private vehicles and **tuk-tuks** are available for local transport.

Best Time to Visit

Visit Jungle Beach**between January and July** for minimum foot traffic, local visitors and great weather.
