What is "Clean Sri Lanka"?

"Clean Sri Lanka" is the newest program launched by the Sri Lankan Government to clean, filter, and enhance the country for a better future. As part of this initiative, streets, roads, and highways are being cleaned, and new laws are being passed to maintain and improve Sri Lanka’s environment.

This program ensures that both locals and tourists enjoy a cleaner and more beautiful Sri Lanka. We kindly encourage you to support this initiative while you explore the island.
