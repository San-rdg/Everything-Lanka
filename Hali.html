Haliela, Badulla, Sri Lanka

Haliela, nestled in the scenic Badulla District, is a picturesque town surrounded by lush greenery, tea plantations, and towering mountains. It’s a tranquil escape for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike.

Top Attractions

  • Visit the **Dunhinda Falls**, one of the most famous waterfalls in Sri Lanka.
  • Explore the **tea estates** and enjoy a walk through the lush plantations.
  • The peaceful **Haliela Temple**, an ancient site offering a serene atmosphere.
  • Panoramic views of the **surrounding hills** and valleys, ideal for photography.

How to Get There

  • Located in the **Badulla District**, approximately 30 km from the town of Badulla.
  • You can take a **train to Badulla Station**, and from there take a short tuk-tuk ride or bus.
  • If traveling by car, **Haliela is easily accessible** via the A5 highway from Badulla.

Best Time to Visit

Visit Haliela from **March to September** when the weather is cool and pleasant, perfect for outdoor activities and sightseeing.

Interesting Facts

  • The **Dunhinda Falls** is one of the tallest and most visited waterfalls in Sri Lanka.
  • Haliela is an area rich in **history and culture**, with ancient temples and religious sites.
  • The surrounding **tea plantations** offer opportunities to explore and learn about tea production.
  • Haliela's natural beauty is a perfect blend of **mountain views, rivers, and forests**.

Whether you seek adventure or simply want to immerse yourself in nature, Haliela is a destination that offers both serenity and excitement.
