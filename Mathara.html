Welcome to Matara

Matara District

Matara is a coastal city in Sri Lanka, known for its natural beauty, beaches, and historical landmarks.

History & Heritage

Matara has a rich colonial and Buddhist heritage, with historic forts and temples adding to its cultural significance.

Natural Beauty

Matara is blessed with pristine beaches, lagoons, and coastal landscapes. The area is known for its serene environment and abundant natural beauty.

Tourist Attractions

  • Matara Fort: A Dutch-era fort with historical architecture and scenic views of the coastline.
  • Weherahena Temple: A Buddhist temple known for its large Buddha statue and peaceful surroundings.
  • Polhena Beach: A popular beach destination known for its calm waters and marine life.
  • Star Fort: A historic fort located in the heart of Matara.
  • Hummanaya Blowhole: A natural sea geyser that attracts visitors from around the world.

Culture & Lifestyle

Matara's culture is influenced by a blend of Buddhist, colonial, and local traditions, making it a vibrant and unique destination for travelers.

Connectivity

Matara is well-connected by road, with direct access to Colombo and other parts of Sri Lanka. It also has a railway station connecting to major cities.

With its stunning beaches, historical landmarks, and rich culture, Matara is a must-visit destination in Sri Lanka.
