Kandy District

A must-see destination with memorable sights and experiences.

Discover Kandy

Kandy is known for its unique blend of local life, natural spots and cultural landmarks.

History & Heritage

This district has historical significance and local traditions that reflect Sri Lanka's diverse past.

Natural Beauty

The area offers scenic views, coastal stretches, reservoirs, and rural landscapes depending on the region.

Top Tourist Attractions

  • Temple of the Tooth (Sri Dalada Maligawa)
  • Royal Botanical Gardens, Peradeniya
  • Kandy Lake
  • Cultural shows
  • Udawattakele Forest Reserve

Connectivity

Accessible by major roads; many districts are served by the national railway and regional bus routes. Nearest airports and major highways vary by district.

Climate & Best Time to Visit

Tropical climate. Check local forecasts — dry months are typically the best time to visit for outdoor activities.

FAQs

How do I get to Kandy?

Most visitors travel by train or bus; private cars and domestic flights (where available) provide the fastest access.

Is it safe to visit?

Generally safe for tourists; follow local guidance, respect customs, and keep basic precautions.
