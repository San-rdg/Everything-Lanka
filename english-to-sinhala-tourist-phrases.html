Frequently asked: quick Sinhala phrase answers

How to say hello in Sinhala?
ආයුබෝවන් (Ayubowan). Use for formal greeting and to wish long life.
How do I pronounce 'ස්තුතියි'?
Estuti — pronounced es-thu-thi. Short and polite for 'thank you'.
What phrase to use when you need help?
උදව් කරන්න! (Udav karanna!) — "Help!"
