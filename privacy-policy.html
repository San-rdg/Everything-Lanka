Privacy Policy

Effective: 16 August 2025 · No email collection

Quick summary: Everything Lanka does not require accounts, does not collect emails, and does not sell or rent your data. Most features run locally in your browser using session/local storage that you control.

1) Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to everythinglanka.wiki and any tools or pages we host under this domain. It explains what we do (and don’t do) with information when you browse our site and use features like the trip planner, story paths, quizzes, badges, weather, and other travel tools.

2) Data we do not collect

  • No accounts, no logins. We do not run user accounts and we do not create personal profiles.
  • No email collection. We do not ask for, store, or require your email address to use the site.
  • No payment details. We do not process payments on this site.
  • No advertising trackers. We do not use behavioral ad networks or cross‑site tracking beacons.

3) What runs locally in your browser

Most of our interactive features work client‑side (on your device) and store lightweight preferences using your browser’s localStorage or sessionStorage. Examples include:

  • Trip Planner selections (vibe, regions, duration) until you generate a plan.
  • Story Path choices, progress badges, and confetti moments.
  • Quiz answers and score history.
  • Weather district preferences and recently viewed districts.
  • Language preferences (Sinhala/Tamil translations toggled on pages).

These values are stored only on your device and are not sent to our servers. You can clear them anytime using the button below or via your browser settings.

How to clear via browser settings →

4) Hosting, security & request logs

Our website is served through common web infrastructure (hosting + CDN). For security and performance, these providers typically keep standard web server logs (for example: IP address, URLs requested, timestamps, and basic device/browser info). We use these logs to operate the site, prevent fraud or abuse, and understand high‑level traffic patterns. We do not use them to identify you personally, and we purge or aggregate them after a short period.

5) Cookies & local storage

  • Cookies: We aim to avoid setting cookies. If a small, essential cookie is ever used (for example to remember a preference), it will not be used for advertising or cross‑site tracking.
  • Local/session storage: Used for on‑device features like planners, badges, quizzes, and language toggles. You can clear these anytime (see controls below).
  • No analytics beacons by default: We currently do not run third‑party analytics scripts that track you across sites.

6) Third‑party services & links

Our pages may link out to third‑party sites (e.g., Instagram, booking partners, official information pages) or load content like images and videos hosted elsewhere. When you interact with those services, their own privacy terms apply. If a page in your browser calls a public API (for example, to fetch weather or scores), your IP address may be visible to that API provider.

7) Uploaded media & meme creator

Our "Lankan Meme Creator" and similar tools are designed to work locally in your browser. Images you upload are processed on your device and are not uploaded to our servers unless we clearly say otherwise on that page. If you choose to download or share a meme, the file stays with you and any sharing is under your control.

8) Ads, sponsorships & affiliates

From time to time, we may feature sponsored experiences or affiliate links. Sponsorships will be labeled (e.g., “Sponsored”). Clicking an external offer takes you to the partner’s site where their privacy policy applies. We do not share your personal data with sponsors.

9) Google Ads & third‑party cookies

When we display Google Ads on this site:

  • Third‑party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.
  • Google’s use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to Everything Lanka and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. Alternatively, you can opt out of a third‑party vendor’s use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info.

10) Your controls

  • Instant reset: Use the Clear my Everything Lanka data button above to wipe local/session storage and non‑critical cookies for this site.
  • Browser settings: You can also clear site data via your browser: Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data (choose “Cookies and other site data” and “Cached images and files”).
  • Feedback removal: If you’ve ever sent us feedback that includes personal information and want it deleted, email us and we’ll erase it.

11) Legal bases & your rights

We design Everything Lanka to minimize personal data processing. Where standard request logs or essential preferences are processed, we rely on our legitimate interests to operate a secure, reliable website. If Sri Lankan data protection law (PDPA) or other laws grant you additional rights (such as access, correction, objection, or deletion), you can exercise them by contacting us. We will honor valid requests and explain anything we don’t control (for example, data that lives only on your device).

12) Children’s privacy

Our content is suitable for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information via feedback, please contact us and we’ll delete it.

13) Changes to this policy

We may update this page to reflect new features or legal requirements. We’ll revise the Effective date at the top and keep changes reasonable and transparent.
