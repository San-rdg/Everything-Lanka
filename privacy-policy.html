Your Privacy Matters to Us

This page explains how EverythingLanka.wiki handles your personal information and privacy.

Information We Collect

When you visit EverythingLanka.wiki, we automatically collect non-personal information such as your IP address, browser type, and general location (city/country level) through cookies and analytics tools. This data helps us improve the website and better understand how visitors use our services.

We do not collect personal information like your name or email address unless you voluntarily provide it through a contact form or similar feature.

Cookies

Our site uses cookies to personalize content and track site analytics. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser at any time.

Use of Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display relevant ads. Google may use cookies to personalize these ads based on your preferences. Learn more from Google's advertising policies.

Your Rights

You can request to view, modify, or delete your personal information by contacting us at info@everythinglanka.wiki.

Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. All changes will be reflected on this page with a revised date.
