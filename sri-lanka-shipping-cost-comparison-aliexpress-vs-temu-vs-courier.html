Frequently Asked Questions

Which platform has the cheapest shipping to Sri Lanka?

Temu often offers cheaper or free shipping for smaller items, while AliExpress provides more variety but may charge for delivery.

Are there hidden courier charges when importing?

Yes. Local delivery companies might add taxes, handling fees, or customs clearance charges at the time of delivery.

Should I avoid DHL or Aramex?

Only use them for valuable or time-sensitive items. They're reliable but expensive and almost always taxed.
