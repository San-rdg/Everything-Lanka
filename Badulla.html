Welcome to Badulla

Badulla District

Badulla is a picturesque town located in the Uva Province of Sri Lanka. Known for its breathtaking landscapes, historical significance, and cultural heritage, Badulla offers a serene retreat in the heart of the island.

History & Heritage

Badulla is steeped in history and is a part of Sri Lanka’s ancient Kingdom of Kandy. It has witnessed various key moments in Sri Lanka’s past, from ancient kingdoms to colonial times, making it a rich source of cultural and historical significance.

Religious Significance

The region is home to various Buddhist temples, including the famous Uva Viharaya, which is an important site for pilgrims. The region’s spiritual landscape reflects a blend of ancient Buddhist influences and local traditions.

Natural Beauty & Agriculture

Badulla is surrounded by lush tea plantations, rivers, and mist-covered mountains. The area’s agricultural significance is notable, especially for its tea production. Badulla’s temperate climate and fertile lands make it a great location for cultivating tea and other crops.

Tourist Attractions

  • Ravana Falls: One of the widest waterfalls in Sri Lanka, located in the dense forests of Badulla.
  • Uva Viharaya: A sacred Buddhist temple with a rich history and beautiful surroundings.
  • Demodara Nine Arches Bridge: A marvel of engineering and one of Sri Lanka's most famous landmarks.
  • Badulla District Court: A colonial-era structure with beautiful architectural details.
  • Haputale: A scenic mountain town offering panoramic views of the surrounding hills and valleys.

Culture & Lifestyle

The people of Badulla live a peaceful and tranquil life, heavily influenced by the local customs and Buddhist teachings. The lifestyle is a harmonious blend of traditional practices and modern living, with local festivals like Sinhala & Tamil New Year being celebrated with great enthusiasm.

Connectivity

Badulla is well connected by road and rail, making it easily accessible from other parts of Sri Lanka, including Colombo and Kandy. The town is a vital part of the Uva Province’s transportation network, with buses and trains serving the area frequently.

Interesting Facts

  • Badulla is located in the Uva Province, which is one of the most scenic regions of Sri Lanka.
  • The famous Demodara Nine Arches Bridge is often referred to as one of the most beautiful railway bridges in the world.
  • Badulla is also known for its annual Badulla Perahera, a grand procession celebrating Sri Lankan culture.
  • The area is home to several endemic species of flora and fauna, especially within its mountainous and forested regions.

Badulla offers a mix of history, culture, and natural beauty, making it an ideal destination for travelers seeking peace, adventure, and a deeper connection with Sri Lanka’s rich heritage.
